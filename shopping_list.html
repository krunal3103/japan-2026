Shiroi Koibito (White Chocolate Cookie Sandwich)
Hokkaido's most famous souvenir — white chocolate cream sandwiched between langue de chat biscuits. One of Japan's best gifts.

Hi-Chew — Japanese Fruit Chews
Japan-only flavours: yogurt, muscat grape, lychee, Japanese peach. The original Japanese versions are noticeably better than export versions.

✓
CONDIMENT
Japanese Curry Paste / S&B Golden Curry
The brick-style curry roux blocks are significantly better in Japan. Also look for Vermont Curry (apple & honey). Perfect for recreating Japanese curry at home.
📍 Supermarkets · Don Quijote
✓
TEA
Ippodo Matcha / Hojicha
Japan's finest matcha tea house. Buy ceremonial grade matcha and hojicha. The Kyoto flagship is in Kyoto; they also have a Tokyo outpost. Comes in beautiful gift tins.
📍 Ippodo Kyoto (Teramachi) · Ippodo Tokyo (Marunouchi)
💄 Beauty & Skincare
✓
SKINCARE
Hada Labo Gokujyun Hyaluronic Lotion
Japan's #1 skincare product. The original formula is significantly cheaper here. Get the blue cap (Original) or gold cap (Premium). Buy multiple.
📍 Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Don Quijote · Sundrug
✓
SUNSCREEN
Biore UV Aqua Rich SPF50+
Japan's most popular sunscreen. Weightless watery texture, no white cast, SPF50+. Much cheaper in Japan. Stock up — it's unavailable or expensive elsewhere.
📍 Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Konbini · Don Quijote
✓
HAIR
Fino Premium Touch Hair Mask
The Reels-famous Japanese hair mask. Deep conditioning, smells amazing. One application noticeably different. Very affordable — buy 2–3 packs.
📍 Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Don Quijote
✓
SHEET MASK
Lululun Sheet Masks (Face Mask)
Japan's iconic daily sheet mask. Regional flavours only available in Japan — Kyoto (white tea), Hokkaido (rose hip). The pink Lululun is the classic. Buy big box packs.
📍 Don Quijote · Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Airports
✓
MAKEUP
Canmake / Cezanne / Kate (Japanese Drugstore Makeup)
Japan's drugstore makeup is world-class. Canmake highlighting powder, Cezanne foundation, Kate brow mascara. All heavily featured on Reels — a fraction of the price vs abroad.
📍 Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Don Quijote · Sundrug
💊 Pharmacy Essentials
✓
PHARMACY
Roihi Tsuboko Pain Patches
Small circular pain relief patches (camphor-based). Apply directly on sore muscles or joints. Super popular on travel Reels — great for tired feet after walking 20,000 steps/day. Perfectly fine in carry-on.
📍 Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Any pharmacy
✓
PHARMACY
Salonpas Pain Relief Patches (Large)
Japan-made Salonpas are significantly stronger than the export version. Larger size, better formula. Great for long-haul flights home and sore legs from the trip.
📍 Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Don Quijote
✓
PHARMACY
Rohto Eye Drops (Platinum / Ice)
Japanese Rohto eye drops are a completely different product from export versions — much stronger cooling effect. Rohto Platinum is the strongest. Will feel intense the first time. Very popular on Reels.
📍 Matsumoto Kiyoshi · Any konbini
👗 Fashion & Accessories

FASHION
Uniqlo Japan-Exclusive Items
Uniqlo Japan has pieces never exported — linen shirts in Japan-specific colours, special collaborations, UTme! custom print T-shirts. The Ginza flagship (12 floors) has the most exclusive range.

ACCESSORIES
Goshuincho Stamp Book
Your goshuin book for collecting temple/shrine stamps throughout the trip. Buy at Senso-ji (Asakusa) or Kiyomizu-dera on arrival — they sell beautiful ones at the temples themselves. Red with gold Kaminarimon design at Senso-ji is iconic.
📍 Senso-ji Temple · Kiyomizu-dera · Stationery shops

Tenugui (Japanese Hand Towel) — Kyoto Print
